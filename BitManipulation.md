## Bitwise Operations

|------------------------------------------------|
|                << Left Shift                   |
|------------------------------------------------|
| 1 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 |       149      |
|------------------------------------------------|
| 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 149 << 1 = 42  |
|------------------------------------------------|
| 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 0 | 0 | 149 << 3 = 168 |
|------------------------------------------------|


|-----------------------------------------------|
|                >> Right Shift                 |
|-----------------------------------------------|
| 1 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 |      149      |
|-----------------------------------------------|
| 0 | 1 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 149 >> 1 = 74 |
|-----------------------------------------------|
| 0 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 149 >> 3 = 18 |
|-----------------------------------------------|
